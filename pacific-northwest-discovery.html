Pacific Northwest Discovery

Duration: 9 Days / 8 Nights


Day 1: Arrival in Seattle

Afternoon:

  • Arrival: Arrive in Seattle and check into your centrally located hotel.
  • Pike Place Market: Begin your adventure with a visit to Pike Place Market. Explore the bustling stalls, sample local delicacies, and watch the famous fish toss.

Evening:

  • Space Needle: Head to the Space Needle for panoramic views of the city and surrounding mountains. Enjoy dinner at the SkyCity Restaurant, if available, or at a nearby restaurant.

Day 2: Seattle

Morning:

  • Chihuly Garden and Glass: Explore the stunning glass sculptures and gardens at Chihuly Garden and Glass.
  • Museum of Pop Culture: Visit the Museum of Pop Culture (MoPOP) to explore exhibits on music, science fiction, and pop culture.

Afternoon:

  • Seattle Waterfront: Stroll along the Seattle Waterfront, visiting attractions like the Seattle Great Wheel and the Seattle Aquarium.

Evening:

  • Ballard Locks: Visit the Ballard Locks to watch boats pass through and see the fish ladder where salmon migrate.

Day 3: Mount Rainier National Park

Morning:

  • Travel to Mount Rainier: Depart from Seattle and head to Mount Rainier National Park.

Afternoon:

  • Paradise Area: Explore the Paradise area of the park, known for its stunning views and wildflower meadows. Take a short hike on the Skyline Trail for breathtaking views of Mount Rainier.

Evening:

  • Overnight in Ashford: Check into your hotel or lodge in Ashford, the gateway to Mount Rainier National Park.

Day 4: Mount Rainier to Olympic National Park

Morning:

  • Reflection Lakes and Narada Falls: Visit Reflection Lakes for iconic views of Mount Rainier mirrored in the water, then continue to Narada Falls for a short hike to the waterfall viewpoint.

Afternoon:

  • Travel to Olympic National Park: Depart from Mount Rainier and travel to Olympic National Park.

Evening:

  • Port Angeles: Check into your hotel in Port Angeles and enjoy dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 5: Olympic National Park - Hurricane Ridge

Morning:

  • Hurricane Ridge: Drive up to Hurricane Ridge for panoramic views of the Olympic Mountains. Enjoy short hikes and wildlife spotting.

Afternoon:

  • Elwha Valley: Explore the Elwha Valley, taking a walk to Madison Falls and learning about the Elwha River restoration project.

Evening:

  • Return to Port Angeles: Return to Port Angeles for an evening of relaxation.

Day 6: Olympic National Park - Hoh Rain Forest and Pacific Coast

Morning:

  • Hoh Rain Forest: Travel to the Hoh Rain Forest, one of the park’s most enchanting areas. Take a walk on the Hall of Mosses and Spruce Nature trails to experience the lush, green environment.

Afternoon:

  • Pacific Coast: Head to the Pacific Coast to explore the rugged beaches. Visit Rialto Beach or Second Beach for a scenic coastal hike.

Evening:

  • Forks: Stay overnight in Forks, a small town near the park’s coastal region.

Day 7: Olympic National Park - Lake Crescent and Sol Duc

Morning:

  • Lake Crescent: Visit the stunning Lake Crescent, known for its clear blue waters. Enjoy a hike to Marymere Falls.

Afternoon:

  • Sol Duc Hot Springs: Relax at Sol Duc Hot Springs, soaking in the natural hot springs and enjoying the serene forest surroundings.

Evening:

  • Return to Port Angeles: Return to Port Angeles for your final night in the area.

Day 8: Seattle to Portland

Morning:

  • Travel to Portland: Depart from Port Angeles and travel to Portland, Oregon.

Afternoon:

  • Powell’s City of Books: Visit Powell’s City of Books, the world’s largest independent bookstore.
  • International Rose Test Garden: Stroll through the International Rose Test Garden, home to thousands of rose varieties.

Evening:

  • Food Cart Dinner: Experience Portland’s renowned food cart scene for dinner, sampling a variety of cuisines.

Day 9: Columbia River Gorge

Morning:

  • Columbia River Gorge: Take a day trip to the Columbia River Gorge. Visit Multnomah Falls, the tallest waterfall in Oregon, and hike to the top if you’re feeling adventurous.

Afternoon:

  • Scenic Drives and Hikes: Enjoy scenic drives along the Historic Columbia River Highway, stopping at viewpoints and smaller waterfalls.

Evening:

  • Return to Portland: Return to Portland for your final evening. Enjoy a farewell dinner at a farm-to-table restaurant.

Included in the Package:
  • Accommodation in centrally located hotels and lodges
  • Meals as specified in the itinerary
  • Transportation between destinations (bus/flight)
  • Entry fees to all national parks and guided tours
  • Professional tour guide throughout the trip
