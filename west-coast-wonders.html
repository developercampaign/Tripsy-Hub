West Coast Wonders

Duration: 10 Days / 9 Nights


Day 1: Arrival in Los Angeles

Afternoon:

  • Arrival: Arrive in Los Angeles and check into your hotel in the heart of the city.
  • Hollywood Tour: Begin your tour with a visit to the Hollywood Walk of Fame, Grauman's Chinese Theatre, and the Hollywood Sign.

Evening:

  • Beverly Hills: Explore the upscale shops and restaurants in Beverly Hills, including Rodeo Drive.
  • Santa Monica Pier: Head to Santa Monica Pier for a sunset stroll and dinner at one of the seaside restaurants.

Day 2: Los Angeles

Morning:

  • Universal Studios Hollywood: Spend the day at Universal Studios Hollywood, experiencing thrilling rides, shows, and a behind-the-scenes look at movie-making.

Evening:

  • Dinner: Enjoy dinner at Universal CityWalk, a lively entertainment district adjacent to the theme park.

Day 3: San Diego

Morning:

  • Travel to San Diego: Depart from Los Angeles and travel to San Diego.

Afternoon:

  • San Diego Zoo: Visit the world-renowned San Diego Zoo, home to thousands of animals and lush, botanical gardens.

Evening:

  • Gaslamp Quarter: Explore the historic Gaslamp Quarter, known for its vibrant nightlife and dining options.

Day 4: San Diego to Las Vegas

Morning:

  • Balboa Park: Spend the morning exploring Balboa Park, home to museums, gardens, and cultural institutions.

Afternoon:

  • Travel to Las Vegas: Travel to Las Vegas by bus or flight, arriving in the late afternoon.

Evening:

  • Las Vegas Strip: Take an evening tour of the Las Vegas Strip, visiting famous casinos and enjoying the dazzling lights and entertainment.

Day 5: Las Vegas

Morning:

  • Grand Canyon West Rim: Take a day trip to the Grand Canyon West Rim. Walk on the Skywalk, a glass bridge extending over the canyon, and enjoy breathtaking views.

Evening:

  • Return to Las Vegas: Return to Las Vegas for an evening of entertainment, such as a Cirque du Soleil show or a concert.

Day 6: Las Vegas to San Francisco

Morning:

  • Relax: Enjoy a leisurely morning in Las Vegas, perhaps lounging by the pool or exploring more of the city.

Afternoon:

  • Travel to San Francisco: Fly to San Francisco and check into your hotel.

Evening:

  • Golden Gate Bridge: Visit the iconic Golden Gate Bridge at sunset for a picturesque view of the bay.

Day 7: San Francisco

Morning:

  • Alcatraz Island: Take a ferry to Alcatraz Island for a guided tour of the historic prison.

Afternoon:

  • Fisherman’s Wharf and Pier 39: Explore Fisherman's Wharf, where you can see sea lions, visit the Aquarium of the Bay, and enjoy fresh seafood.

Evening:

  • Cable Car Ride: Ride one of San Francisco’s famous cable cars through the city’s hilly streets.

Day 8: San Francisco to Yosemite National Park

Morning:

  • Travel to Yosemite: Depart early for Yosemite National Park.

Afternoon:

  • Yosemite Valley: Arrive at Yosemite National Park and begin exploring Yosemite Valley, seeing landmarks such as El Capitan, Half Dome, and Yosemite Falls.

Evening:

  • Lodge Stay: Check into a lodge within or near the park and enjoy dinner surrounded by nature.

Day 9: Yosemite National Park

Morning:

  • Hiking and Nature Walks: Spend the day hiking and exploring Yosemite’s trails, with options for all skill levels.

Afternoon:

  • Scenic Views: Continue to explore the park’s stunning landscapes, such as Glacier Point and Mariposa Grove of Giant Sequoias.

Evening:

  • Relax: Return to your lodge for a relaxing evening.

Day 10: Departure from San Francisco

Morning:

  • Return to San Francisco: Travel back to San Francisco.

Afternoon:

  • Free Time: Enjoy some free time for last-minute shopping or sightseeing.

Evening:

  • Departure: Check out of your hotel and depart from San Francisco, concluding your West Coast Wonders adventure.

Included in the Package:
  • Accommodation in centrally located hotels and lodges
  • Meals as specified in the itinerary
  • Transportation between cities (bus/flight)
  • Entry fees to all attractions and guided tours
  • Evening entertainment (shows, cruises, etc.)
  • Professional tour guide throughout the trip
