New England Fall Foliage Tour

Duration: 8 Days / 7 Nights


Day 1: Arrival in Boston

Afternoon:

  • Arrival: Arrive in Boston and check into your centrally located hotel.
  • Freedom Trail: Begin your journey with a walk along the Freedom Trail, visiting historic sites such as Boston Common, Paul Revere's House, and the Old North Church.

Evening:

  • Welcome Dinner: Enjoy a welcome dinner at a local restaurant, introducing you to the flavors of New England cuisine.

Day 2: Boston to Stowe, Vermont

Morning:

  • Boston Sightseeing: Visit the Boston Public Garden and the historic Beacon Hill neighborhood.
  • Travel to Stowe: Depart from Boston and travel to Stowe, Vermont, known for its charming village and scenic beauty.

Afternoon:

  • Stowe Village: Arrive in Stowe and explore the picturesque village, visiting local shops and cafes.

Evening:

  • Overnight in Stowe: Check into your hotel or lodge and enjoy a relaxing evening.

Day 3: Stowe and Surrounding Area

Morning:

  • Mount Mansfield: Take a scenic drive or hike up Mount Mansfield, Vermont’s highest peak, for breathtaking views of the fall foliage.

Afternoon:

  • Ben & Jerry’s Factory Tour: Visit the nearby Ben & Jerry’s Ice Cream Factory in Waterbury for a fun and tasty tour.

Evening:

  • Dinner in Stowe: Enjoy dinner at a cozy restaurant in Stowe, featuring local Vermont specialties.

Day 4: Stowe to White Mountains, New Hampshire

Morning:

  • Travel to White Mountains: Depart from Stowe and travel to the White Mountains of New Hampshire.

Afternoon:

  • Kancamagus Highway: Drive along the Kancamagus Highway, one of New England’s most scenic routes, renowned for its stunning fall colors.
  • Flume Gorge: Explore Flume Gorge, a natural gorge extending 800 feet at the base of Mount Liberty.

Evening:

  • Overnight in North Conway: Check into your hotel in North Conway and enjoy an evening at your leisure.

Day 5: White Mountains

Morning:

  • Mount Washington: Visit the Mount Washington Auto Road or take the cog railway to the summit of Mount Washington, the highest peak in the Northeastern US, for panoramic views.

Afternoon:

  • Franconia Notch State Park: Explore Franconia Notch State Park, including the Basin, a beautiful natural pool, and the Cannon Mountain Aerial Tramway for more spectacular views.

Evening:

  • Dinner in North Conway: Enjoy dinner at a local restaurant in North Conway.

Day 6: White Mountains to the Berkshires, Massachusetts

Morning:

  • Travel to the Berkshires: Depart from the White Mountains and travel to the Berkshires in Western Massachusetts.

Afternoon:

  • Norman Rockwell Museum: Visit the Norman Rockwell Museum in Stockbridge to see the works of the famous American illustrator.

Evening:

  • Overnight in the Berkshires: Check into your hotel or inn in the Berkshires and relax for the evening.

Day 7: The Berkshires

Morning:

  • Hiking: Enjoy a morning hike in the Berkshires, such as Monument Mountain or Mount Greylock, the highest point in Massachusetts.

Afternoon:

  • Historic Homes: Visit historic homes like The Mount, Edith Wharton's home, or Naumkeag, a Gilded Age mansion with beautiful gardens.

Evening:

  • Dinner in Lenox: Have dinner in the charming town of Lenox, known for its cultural and culinary delights.

Day 8: Berkshires to Boston

Morning:

  • Scenic Drive: Take a scenic drive back to Boston, enjoying the final glimpses of fall foliage.

Afternoon:

  • Free Time in Boston: Spend your final afternoon in Boston exploring at your leisure, perhaps visiting the Boston Public Library or shopping on Newbury Street.

Evening:

  • Departure: Check out of your hotel and depart from Boston, concluding your New England Fall Foliage Tour.

Included in the Package:
  • Accommodation in centrally located hotels and lodges
  • Meals as specified in the itinerary
  • Transportation between destinations (bus/flight)
  • Entry fees to all attractions and guided tours
  • Professional tour guide throughout the trip
