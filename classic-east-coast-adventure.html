Classic East Coast Adventure

Duration: 7 Days / 6 Nights


Day 1: Arrival in New York City

Afternoon:

  • Arrival: Arrive in New York City and check into your centrally located hotel.

  • Welcome Dinner: Enjoy a welcome dinner at a local restaurant, giving you a taste of NYC’s diverse culinary scene.

Evening:

  • City Tour: Begin with an evening city tour. Visit Times Square, where the bright lights and bustling energy set the tone for your adventure.

  • Empire State Building: Ascend the Empire State Building for a stunning view of the city skyline at night.


Day 2: New York City Sightseeing

Morning:

  • Statue of Liberty & Ellis Island: Start your day with a ferry ride to Liberty Island to see the iconic Statue of Liberty. Continue to Ellis Island to explore the immigration museum.

Afternoon:

  • Central Park: Stroll through Central Park, visiting landmarks such as Bethesda Terrace and the Bow Bridge.

  • Museum Mile: Visit one of the world-renowned museums along Museum Mile, such as the Metropolitan Museum of Art or the Guggenheim Museum.

Evening:

  • Broadway Show: Experience the magic of Broadway with a live theater performance in the famous Theater District.


Day 3: Philadelphia

Morning:

  • Travel to Philadelphia: Depart from New York City and travel to Philadelphia.

Afternoon:

  • Historical Sites: Visit Independence Hall, where the Declaration of Independence and the Constitution were debated and adopted. View the iconic Liberty Bell.

  • Reading Terminal Market: Enjoy lunch at the Reading Terminal Market, sampling local foods like Philly cheesesteaks and Amish pretzels.


Day 4: Washington D.C.

Morning:

  • Travel to Washington D.C.: Depart from Philadelphia and arrive in Washington D.C., the nation’s capital.

Afternoon:

  • National Mall: Explore the National Mall, visiting landmarks such as the Lincoln Memorial, Washington Monument, and the U.S. Capitol.

  • Smithsonian Museums: Choose from a variety of Smithsonian museums to visit, including the National Museum of American History, the National Air and Space Museum, or the National Museum of Natural History.

Evening:

  • Georgetown: Explore the historic Georgetown neighborhood, with its cobblestone streets, boutique shops, and waterfront dining options.


Day 5: Washington D.C.

Morning:

  • Arlington National Cemetery: Visit Arlington National Cemetery to see the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier and the Eternal Flame at John F. Kennedy’s gravesite.

Afternoon:

  • National Archives: View the original Declaration of Independence, Constitution, and Bill of Rights at the National Archives.


Day 6: Boston

Morning:

  • Travel to Boston: Depart from Washington D.C. and travel to Boston.

Afternoon:

  • Freedom Trail: Walk the Freedom Trail, a 2.5-mile route that leads you to 16 historical sites, including Boston Common, Paul Revere's House, and the Old North Church.

  • Harvard University: Visit Harvard University in nearby Cambridge, where you can take a guided tour of the historic campus.

Evening:

  • Boston Harbor: Enjoy dinner at a seafood restaurant along the Boston Harbor, known for its fresh catches and scenic views.


Day 7: Departure from Boston

Morning:

  • Free Time: Spend your final morning in Boston exploring at your leisure. Consider visiting the New England Aquarium or shopping for souvenirs at Quincy Market.

  • Departure: Check out of your hotel and depart from Boston, taking with you unforgettable memories of your Classic East Coast Adventure.


Included in the Package:

  • Accommodation in centrally located hotels

  • Meals as specified in the itinerary

  • Transportation between cities (train/bus/flight)

  • Entry fees to all attractions and guided tours

  • Evening entertainment (Broadway show, jazz cruise, etc.)

  • Professional tour guide throughout the trip
