National Parks Expedition

Duration: 12 Days / 11 Nights


Day 1: Arrival in Denver

Afternoon:

  • Arrival: Arrive in Denver and check into your centrally located hotel.
  • Welcome Dinner: Enjoy a welcome dinner at a local restaurant, introducing you to the flavors of the Rocky Mountains.

Evening:

  • Free Time: Relax and explore downtown Denver at your leisure.

Day 2: Rocky Mountain National Park

Morning:

  • Travel to Rocky Mountain National Park: Depart from Denver and head to Rocky Mountain National Park.

Afternoon:

  • Scenic Drives and Hikes: Explore the park’s stunning landscapes, including scenic drives along Trail Ridge Road and short hikes to viewpoints like Bear Lake and Sprague Lake.

Evening:

  • Overnight in Estes Park: Check into your hotel or lodge in Estes Park, the gateway to Rocky Mountain National Park.

Day 3: Denver to Moab

Morning:

  • Return to Denver: Head back to Denver in the morning.

Afternoon:

  • Travel to Moab: Drive or take a short flight to Moab, Utah, the base for exploring Arches and Canyonlands National Parks.

Evening:

  • Free Time: Check into your hotel and enjoy the evening exploring the charming town of Moab.

Day 4: Arches National Park

Morning:

  • Arches National Park: Spend the day exploring Arches National Park, home to over 2,000 natural sandstone arches. Key sights include Delicate Arch, Landscape Arch, and Balanced Rock.

Afternoon:

  • Hiking: Take short hikes to various viewpoints and arches, and enjoy a picnic lunch in the park.

Evening:

  • Return to Moab: Return to Moab for dinner and relaxation.

Day 5: Canyonlands National Park

Morning:

  • Canyonlands National Park: Explore the Island in the Sky district of Canyonlands National Park. Visit the Grand View Point Overlook, Mesa Arch, and Green River Overlook.

Afternoon:

  • Scenic Drives: Enjoy scenic drives and short hikes to panoramic viewpoints.

Evening:

  • Sunset Views: Watch the sunset from Dead Horse Point State Park, which offers stunning views of the Colorado River and Canyonlands.

Day 6: Moab to Bryce Canyon

Morning:

  • Travel to Bryce Canyon: Depart from Moab and travel to Bryce Canyon National Park.

Afternoon:

  • Bryce Canyon National Park: Arrive at Bryce Canyon and begin exploring the park’s unique hoodoo formations.

Evening:

  • Sunset at Bryce Point: Enjoy the sunset at Bryce Point, offering breathtaking views of the colorful amphitheater.

Day 7: Bryce Canyon National Park

Morning:

  • Hiking: Spend the day hiking through Bryce Canyon. Popular trails include the Navajo Loop, Queen’s Garden, and the Rim Trail.

Afternoon:

  • Ranger Programs: Participate in ranger-led programs to learn more about the park’s geology and ecology.

Evening:

  • Stargazing: Experience the night sky at Bryce Canyon, known for its dark skies and excellent stargazing opportunities.

Day 8: Zion National Park

Morning:

  • Travel to Zion National Park: Depart from Bryce Canyon and head to Zion National Park.

Afternoon:

  • The Narrows: Hike the Narrows, a stunning slot canyon where you walk through the Virgin River.

Evening:

  • Springdale: Check into your hotel in Springdale, just outside the park, and enjoy dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 9: Zion National Park

Morning:

  • Hiking: Explore more of Zion with hikes to popular spots like Angels Landing, Emerald Pools, and Observation Point.

Afternoon:

  • Scenic Drives: Take a scenic drive through the Zion-Mount Carmel Highway, featuring impressive tunnels and viewpoints.

Evening:

  • Relax: Return to Springdale for an evening of relaxation.

Day 10: Grand Canyon National Park

Morning:

  • Travel to Grand Canyon: Depart from Zion and travel to the Grand Canyon National Park’s South Rim.

Afternoon:

  • South Rim Exploration: Explore the South Rim’s viewpoints, including Mather Point, Yavapai Observation Station, and Desert View Watchtower.

Evening:

  • Sunset Views: Watch the sunset over the Grand Canyon, a truly awe-inspiring experience.

Day 11: Grand Canyon to Monument Valley

Morning:

  • Sunrise at the Grand Canyon: Catch the sunrise over the Grand Canyon, then take a leisurely hike along the Rim Trail.

Afternoon:

  • Travel to Monument Valley: Head to Monument Valley, a region known for its iconic sandstone buttes.

Evening:

  • Guided Tour: Take a guided tour of Monument Valley, learning about its cultural significance and enjoying the dramatic landscapes.

Day 12: Monument Valley to Mesa Verde National Park

Morning:

  • Travel to Mesa Verde: Depart from Monument Valley and travel to Mesa Verde National Park.

Afternoon:

  • Cliff Dwellings: Explore the ancient cliff dwellings of Mesa Verde, such as Cliff Palace, Balcony House, and Spruce Tree House.

Evening:

  • Departure from Durango: Travel to Durango for your departure. Reflect on the incredible journey through some of the most beautiful national parks in the United States.

Included in the Package:
  • Accommodation in centrally located hotels and lodges
  • Meals as specified in the itinerary
  • Transportation between destinations (bus/flight)
  • Entry fees to all national parks and guided tours
  • Professional tour guide throughout the trip
