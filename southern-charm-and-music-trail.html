Southern Charm and Music Trail

Duration: 8 Days / 7 Nights


Day 1: Arrival in Nashville

Afternoon:

  • Arrival: Arrive in Nashville and check into your downtown hotel.
  • Country Music Hall of Fame: Begin your journey with a visit to the Country Music Hall of Fame and Museum, exploring exhibits on country music’s history and legends.

Evening:

  • Live Music: Enjoy dinner at a local restaurant, followed by live music at a famous honky-tonk on Broadway, such as Tootsie’s Orchid Lounge.

Day 2: Nashville

Morning:

  • Grand Ole Opry: Tour the legendary Grand Ole Opry, where country music’s biggest stars have performed.

Afternoon:

  • Johnny Cash Museum: Visit the Johnny Cash Museum, dedicated to the life and music of the "Man in Black."

Evening:

  • Bluebird Cafe: Experience an intimate performance at the Bluebird Cafe, known for its singer-songwriter showcases.

Day 3: Memphis

Morning:

  • Travel to Memphis: Depart from Nashville and travel to Memphis.

Afternoon:

  • Graceland: Tour Graceland, the home of Elvis Presley, exploring the mansion, gardens, and car museum.

Evening:

  • Beale Street: Head to Beale Street for dinner and an evening of blues music at iconic clubs like B.B. King’s Blues Club.

Day 4: Memphis to New Orleans

Morning:

  • National Civil Rights Museum: Visit the National Civil Rights Museum, located at the Lorraine Motel, where Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. was assassinated.

Afternoon:

  • Travel to New Orleans: Travel to New Orleans by bus or flight, arriving in the late afternoon.

Evening:

  • Jazz Cruise: Enjoy a jazz dinner cruise on the Mississippi River, experiencing live music and Southern cuisine.

Day 5: New Orleans

Morning:

  • French Quarter: Take a walking tour of the historic French Quarter, visiting Jackson Square, St. Louis Cathedral, and the French Market.

Afternoon:

  • Mardi Gras World: Tour Mardi Gras World to see the elaborate floats and learn about the history of this famous festival.

Evening:

  • Bourbon Street: Explore the vibrant nightlife of Bourbon Street, sampling local cocktails and live music.

Day 6: New Orleans

Morning:

  • Swamp Tour: Take a guided swamp tour to see the unique wildlife and landscape of Louisiana’s bayous.

Afternoon:

  • Cooking Class: Participate in a Creole cuisine cooking class, learning to make traditional dishes like gumbo and jambalaya.

Evening:

  • Dinner: Enjoy the meal you’ve prepared during the cooking class or dine at a renowned New Orleans restaurant.

Day 7: Savannah

Morning:

  • Travel to Savannah: Fly to Savannah, Georgia.

Afternoon:

  • Historic District: Take a guided tour of Savannah’s Historic District, with its charming squares, antebellum architecture, and moss-draped oaks.

Evening:

  • River Street: Stroll along River Street, visiting boutiques, galleries, and restaurants with views of the Savannah River.

Day 8: Departure from Savannah

Morning:

  • Free Time: Spend your final morning in Savannah exploring at your leisure. Consider visiting Forsyth Park or the Telfair Museums.

Afternoon:

  • Departure: Check out of your hotel and depart from Savannah, concluding your Southern Charm and Music Trail adventure.

Included in the Package:
  • Accommodation in centrally located hotels
  • Meals as specified in the itinerary
  • Transportation between cities (bus/flight)
  • Entry fees to all attractions and guided tours
  • Evening entertainment (live music, cruises, etc.)
  • Professional tour guide throughout the trip
